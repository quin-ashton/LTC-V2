# WHAT IS LINK TO CHARITY?

Link to Charity (LTC) is a not-for-profit initiative that turns everyday online shopping into charitable donations.

Most online retailers already pay affiliate commissions.

Normally these commissions are paid to:

* Influencers
* Coupon websites
* Review websites
* Marketing companies

Link to Charity redirects those commissions towards charitable causes.

Instead of a marketing company receiving the commission, Link to Charity receives it and donates it.

The shopper pays absolutely nothing extra.

The retailer pays absolutely nothing extra.

The affiliate commission already exists.

Link to Charity simply changes where it goes.

# THE USER JOURNEY

The entire platform revolves around this process:

STEP 1

User visits Link to Charity.

STEP 2

User searches for a retailer.

Examples:

Amazon

Nike

Booking.com

Dell

Trip.com

Cotton On

Thousands of others.

STEP 3

User clicks the retailer.

STEP 4

Link to Charity affiliate tracking activates.

STEP 5

User is redirected to retailer.

STEP 6

User shops normally.

STEP 7

Retailer pays affiliate commission.

STEP 8

Link to Charity receives commission.

STEP 9

Commission is donated to charity.

STEP 10

The impact is displayed publicly on the website.

This process must be explained repeatedly throughout the website because it is the core concept.
